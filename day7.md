Day 7 – Log Analysis Basics

 What I Learned
- Logs store system activity
- Logs help detect suspicious behavior
- SOC analysts use logs for investigations

 Important Logs
- /var/log/syslog: system activity
- /var/log/auth.log: login attempts

 Commands I Used
- cat: view logs
- less: scroll through logs

 SOC Perspective
- Logs show login attempts and errors
- Multiple failed logins may indicate an attack

 My Thoughts
- Logs are very important for cybersecurity
- I want to learn how to analyze them deeply
